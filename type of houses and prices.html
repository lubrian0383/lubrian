TYPES OF BUILDINGS CHARACTERICTICS AND PRICES

BUNGALOW

Bungalows are ground level houses that are one of the oldest forms of housing in Nigeria.

price 25.000.000 million naira

DUPLEX

A Duplex is a kind of building that has a ground floor and additional floors above with more ample space and views.

Price 80.000.000 million naira

TERRACE BUILDING

Terrace buildings A fully detached duplex has its own compound and none of its walls attached to another unit.

It has the utmost amount of privacy and wiggle room.

But it costs more than the terrace or semi-detached duplex.

price 170.000.000 million naira

A MANSION

A mansion is a very large spacious house with several rooms.

It is also referred to as a villa, with the official presidency residence called; Aso villa.

price 200.000.000 million naira

APARTMENT

An apartment building is a block of flats with several floors

and living several units each on an individual floor.

price 600.000.000 million naira

CASTLE

Castles were built surrounded by a water body for kings and queens
They are old buildings and are now preserved as heritage buildings.

price 1.200.000.000 billion naira
